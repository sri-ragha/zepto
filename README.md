# Zepto Logic - Building the Future of Semiconductor Innovation

Zepto Logic Technologies Private Limited is a dynamic fabless semiconductor design house specializing in the complete VLSI design flow, from initial specifications to final GDSII.

## 🚀 Vision & Strategic Pillars

We are building the future of deep tech by focusing on five interconnected strategic pillars:
- **Test IP Creation**: Modular Validation Systems
- **Global Design Services**: Semiconductor Collaboration
- **Commercial IP Development**: Connectivity & Analog
- **Hybrid SoCs of the Future**: Chiplet Architecture & AI
- **Innovation Human Capital**: Knowledge & Growth

- **RTL Design & Synthesis**

- **ASIC Design**
- **Physical Design**
- **Verification & Validation (UVM)**
