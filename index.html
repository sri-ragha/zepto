Evolution of Zepto

Zepto Logic Technologies Private Limited has been part of a national effort to achieve self-reliance in semiconductors since 2018. National policies recognize that chips underpin every electronic product and offer support across the entire design lifecycle. Zepto Logic Technologies is a dynamic fabless semiconductor design house specializing in the complete VLSI design flow, from initial specifications to final GDSII. We are actively building a robust portfolio of reusable Intellectual Property (IP) and firmly believe that with expert architecture and design, open-source EDA ecosystems can yield world-class silicon.

We're committed to nurturing local talent in chip design, artificial intelligence and quantum technologies through deep R&D, mentorship and collaboration. We position ourselves as a we are a Deep Tech company and call for the creation of a vibrant chip-design ecosystem.

Zepto Logic Technologies is also strategically operating a World-Class VLSI & Embedded Training Centre in Coimbatore. This initiative directly addresses the acute national shortage of specialized talent.

Zepto Logic is uniquely positioned to provide full-suite VLSI design services and actively develop indigenous IP built around CDAC Vega and IIT-Madras Shakti RISC-V processors. Utilizing our existing, state-of-the-art infrastructure, laboratory setup, and software, we ensure our training programs are informed by real-time design challenges and conducted to the highest industry standard.
